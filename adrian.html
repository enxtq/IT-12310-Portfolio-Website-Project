About Me

I'm a passionate game designer with a deep love for interactive world-building. My journey started in Roblox Studio at age 14, scripting mini-games for friends — and evolved into a serious professional pursuit of game design craft.

I specialize in creating engaging gameplay loops, intuitive level design, and polished experiences that keep players coming back. From solo projects to collaborating with teams, I bring both creative vision and technical execution to every build.

Currently looking for opportunities to join a studio or take on freelance contracts where I can contribute to larger-scale projects while growing my independent portfolio.
